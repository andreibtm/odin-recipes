Description

Enjoy a delicious homemade pizza with your favorite toppings. This easy recipe will guide you through
making the perfect pizza crust and sauce from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes, or until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat.
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a greased pizza pan or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  9. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
